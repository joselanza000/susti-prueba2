Oluwakemi Adabonyan - M.Sc. Embedded Systems

I am a undertaking a Masters program in Embedded Systems at Oakland University, having completed my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Electronics Engineering from Bells University of Technology, Ota, in Nigeria. Through course work and projects at Oakland, I have acquired knowledge and developed passion for Embedded Computing, exploiting Microprocessors, FPGAs and GPUs. This motivated me to pursue a masters thesis centered on utilizing FPGA architecture and reconfigurable computing for Edge Detection. I am also expanding my expertise in embedded software programming through an internship that exposes me to automotive software development and product processes.
